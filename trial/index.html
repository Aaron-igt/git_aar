Club history

chelsea is a club with a lot of rich history since it's establishment back in 1905. The blues have won around 36 trophies, with 18 of these won during the former club owner and renowned Israeli-Russian businessman and billionaire,Roman Abramovich. Chelsea have won the following trophies:
   6 league trophies
   2 champions league trophies
   2 time UEFA Europa league winners
   2 UEFA cup winners cup trophies
   8 FA cups
   5 league cups
   4 FA community shields
and among others.

Brief Info about some of the club Legends

Frank Lampard
He is widely regarded as one of Chelsea's greatest players ever, and one of the greatest midfielders of his generation. He's chelsea's all time goalscorer with a total of 211 goals since joining Chelsea back in 2001.
Didier Drogba
He was an Ivorian striker who joined chelsea when he was 26 years old from french club, Marseille FC. He scored 164 goals in 381 appearances for chelsea.
Eden Hazard
He's known by most as arguably the greatest player to ever play for Chelsea Football Club. He joined Chelsea Football Club from Losc Lille. He went on to score 110 goals and create 92 goals in 351 games he played for Chelsea before his departure to join Real Madrid in 2019.
